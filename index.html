About Us

A Passion for
Piano, Perfected

M&M Piano was co-founded by a sister and brother who share an unwavering love for music and a commitment to nurturing that same love in others. The name itself reflects their partnership — M&M — a bond built on harmony both in life and in music.

With close to a decade of hands-on performance and teaching experience, our lead instructor brings not only technical mastery but a genuine joy for the craft. Having served as a pianist across three choirs, the musicianship here goes far beyond the keys — it is deeply rooted in ensemble, theory, and expressive playing.

Together, M&M Piano has become a warm, professional, and inspiring space where students of every level are empowered to find their voice at the piano.

Lead Instructor
Minh
Lead Instructor & Co-Founder · Pianist

My journey with the piano began the way many great things do — not by choice, but by chance. At eight years old, during a summer visit to Vietnam to see family, my cousin's love of music caught my parents' attention, and before long I found myself sitting at a piano bench for the very first time. I won't pretend I enjoyed it at first. Classical lessons during a one-month summer stay felt more like a chore than a calling. But music has a way of finding you when you least expect it.

Over the years, something shifted. Through countless hours of practice, the frustration gave way to fascination — and then to genuine love. I discovered a sense of peace at the keys that I hadn't found anywhere else. After about three years of classical training, I began branching out on my own into modern music and music theory, largely through self-study. That independence pushed me to dig deeper, think critically, and develop a musical voice that was entirely my own.

Today, nearly a decade into my journey, I serve as pianist for three choirs and perform regularly at shows, gigs, weddings, funerals, and a wide variety of events. I am currently a junior in high school, and I continue to learn something new with every performance and every practice session — because I firmly believe there is always room to grow.

What drives me most is the look on people's faces when music moves them. There is no greater honor than playing for others and knowing that the same joy I found in this instrument has found its way to them too. That is why I teach — and why I love every moment of it.

What We Offer

Tailored for
Every Level

Whether you have never touched a piano or are looking to elevate your playing to the next level, M&M Piano offers structured, supportive, and personally tailored instruction. Every lesson is crafted around your goals, your pace, and your musical interests.

🎹
Beginner Foundations

Perfect for those brand new to the piano. We build strong fundamentals — hand position, reading notation, rhythm, and basic music theory — in a patient, encouraging environment.

🎵
Technique & Theory

For intermediate players ready to refine their playing. We focus on scales, chord progressions, sight-reading, and the music theory behind what you play — unlocking deeper understanding.

🎼
Advanced Repertoire

For experienced pianists aiming to master challenging pieces, explore different styles, or develop expressive artistry. Includes nuanced interpretation, dynamics, and performance preparation.

Service Area

Based in San Jose, California

M&M Piano operates exclusively as an in-person, on-site studio. All lessons take place at our San Jose location, allowing us to provide a focused, distraction-free learning environment with professional equipment.

At this time, we are unable to offer remote or travel-based instruction. If you reside outside of the San Jose area or are not able to visit us in person, we regret that we cannot accommodate your request — though we hope the distance does not stand between you and your musical journey for long.
